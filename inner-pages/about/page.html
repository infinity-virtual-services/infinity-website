Our Story

Founded from TrioSecure in 2013, we grew from a tech-telecom pioneer into a global operator with 2014 expansions to Hong Kong and Dubai. In 2022, IVS launched as a focused outsourcing and business solutions firm, scaling a small team into a high-impact organization. By 2025, IVS and TrioSecure joined BrainCode Global Group, uniting expertise and accelerating our international reach and innovation.

Get Started

2013

Our journey began in 2013 with TrioSecure — a true pioneer in Tech and Telecom — driven by the ambition to build the impossible at a time when specialized resources were only beginning to emerge. From the very start, our vision was clear: to bridge innovation with reliability, and to shape the future of connectivity through bold ideas and relentless dedication.

2014

In 2014, TrioSecure took a bold step forward with its expansion to Hong Kong and Dubai, marking the beginning of its international journey. These strategic moves not only strengthened the company's global footprint but also positioned it at the crossroads of two major innovation hubs.

2022

IVS launched in 2022, beginning its journey as a tech startup with an initial team of just a few passionate innovators. What started as a small, dedicated group with a shared vision quickly evolved into a dynamic force, driven by creativity, collaboration, and the pursuit of cutting-edge solutions.

2025

In 2025, a strategic partnership between IVS and TrioSecure culminated in their integration into BrainCode Global Group. This milestone marked the beginning of a powerful new chapter — one focused on uniting expertise, expanding global reach, and driving the next wave of technological innovation and success.
