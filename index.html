Our Flagship A.I Products Built for Impact

Valkyrie — The A.I Call Evaluator

Inspired by precision and fairness, Valkyrie listens, analyzes, and scores every recorded sales or support call — instantly. Using advanced speech recognition and language models, it applies your scorecards, identifies improvement areas, and delivers unbiased, data-backed evaluations.

Ideal for: Call centers, sales teams, and customer support operations.

Result: 100% coverage, zero human bias, and hours of QA time saved.

Learn More

Ven — The A.I Sales Agent

Ven (short for "Vendor") is our human-like A.I calling agent that revolutionizes lead conversion. It automatically dials thousands of leads, holds natural conversations, qualifies prospects, and even closes sales using realistic speech synthesis.

Ideal for: Insurance companies and high-volume outbound sales teams.

Result: 10x more calls, consistent follow-up, and round-the-clock performance.

1

Valkyrie analyzes every customer interaction in real-time, ensuring quality standards and detecting potential issues before they escalate.

2

Ven handles your entire sales pipeline autonomously, from initial lead qualification to closing deals, with human-level conversation skills.
